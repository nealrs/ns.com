StoryScroll turns webpages & blog posts into auto-scrolling videos perfectly perfectly sized for Instagram Stories, Posts, and other social media!

Why is this useful?

You can't post links on Instagram unless you're verified or have 10,000 followers. And even then, every link is a roadblock.

Writers, bloggers, and content marketers are at a disadvantage on social media, because these platforms prioritize video and advertisements.

StoryScroll turns your longform content into auto-scrolling videos. No clicks, no distracting sound, no friction. Just a clean, lean-back, engaging & informative experience.

Your videos will look great on Instagram Stories, as posts, and on any social media that supports video.

How does it work?

1. Input a URL

2. Click the Make Videos button

3. Post your videos!

StoryScroll produces three types of videos:

Story: 1080x1920px tall videos, in 60 second segments

Instagram splits each segment into 15 second clips, giving you an opportunity to annotate each clip with stickers, CTAs, or polls.

Another nice feature. is that when you hold down on the screen, the video pauses. Lift your finger up and the video resumes. It's a really nice mechanic for readers.

Post: 1080px square videos, in 60 second segments

Post up to 10 at once, with all your #hashtags, @mentions, and inspo fuel.

Square: full-length 1080px square

Post this one on LinkedIn, Facebook, Twitter, or any other social media app.
